# Welcome to Insights

This is my personal knowledge platform where I document everything I learn — from cybersecurity techniques to software architecture patterns. The goal is simple: if I learn something worth remembering, I write it here.

## Why I Built This

After years of bookmarking articles and scribbling notes in scattered documents, I realized I needed a structured space that was:

- **Searchable** — find anything I've written quickly
- **Shareable** — send links instead of screenshots
- **Persistent** — content I own, not locked behind a platform

## What You'll Find Here

### Cybersecurity
Deep dives into offensive and defensive security — SOC operations, penetration testing methodologies, CTF writeups, and incident response playbooks.

### Development
My explorations in full-stack development, system design, and the tooling I actually use in production.

### Learning Notes
Raw notes from courses, certifications, and books — distilled into actionable summaries.

## How to Follow Along

You can subscribe via the **Follow** button on the homepage (Google Groups), or check back whenever you like. Comments are powered by GitHub Discussions via Giscus, so feel free to engage.

Let's learn together.
